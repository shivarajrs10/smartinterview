Welcome to Smart interviewer 2.0

Instructions

1. Please make sure that you have received both template files in csv format from hiring manager.
2. Please prepare the resume in docx, txt and pdf format only. The name of the file should be same as candidate's name.
3. Click on Log in tab and use your credentials to login.
4. Once you are signed in Profile page will open by default. In that page you are required to upload the following:
-Candidates Resume
-KeyWord Template (Containing keywords in csv format)
-Q & A Template (Containing Question and Answers in csv format)
5. Click on Submit and wait till “Successfully uploaded Please click on Resume score” message appears.
6. Click on "Resume Score” tab, and under dropdown menu "Select header" select appropriate option if you have any preference or select "all".
7. Under the dropdown option of "Select no of candidate" please select number of candidates you want to shortlist for online test.
8. The score in the tabular format will be displayed for your reference.
9. You can send the automatic invitation email to candidate by clicking on the "send" button which includes instructions, login credentials, test links, do's and dont's.
10. When the candidate completes the online test the result will be recorded and shown in the “Quiz Score” page. Reject / Select the candidate for next round
